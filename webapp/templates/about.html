Agile 2911 Group 2

The Agile 2911 Group 2 is comprised of BCIT CIT program students. Our game is a recreation of the popular Plants vs Zombies game, originally created by PopCap games. In our version, we will be including additional entities (both plants and zombies) that never made it into the original game as well as incorporate some base game features. We will also be programming a website and database that will keep track of player scores.
